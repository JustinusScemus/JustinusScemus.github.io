HKUST Anthem

Entry by Lok Hei Justin SHAM, an adaptation from the Mamas and the Papas' California Dreamin'

Stanza 1:
All the views are great, and the labs are good,
I've been for a walk, *in the knowledge woods,
We're all working hard, in HKUST,
Everybody's dreaming, in this university! (Everybody's dream university!)

Stanza 2:
In the library, I'd find a very quiet seat,
Well, I'd get to have a book (get to have a book), and then I'll start to read, (I begin to read,)
You know the people like the vibes, they know you're gonna stay,
Everybody's dreaming, in this university! (Everybody's dream university!)

Stanza 3:
All the views are great, and the labs are good,
I've been for a walk, *in the knowledge woods,
You'd be working hard, in HKUST,
Everybody's dreaming, in this university! (Dreaming university!)
In HKUST! (HKUST!)

* Upon submission of the entry as this PDF file, I did not think that "in" the knowledge woods would be a better choice.
